Moa Adventure

"Myth of Aquatics · Adventure" is a blockchain mobile game which entertains and educates. We hope to increase their understanding towards environmental pollution via exploratory appealing game chapters’ stories to achieve online experiential learning. Thus, we could inspire children to consider further integrating sustainable development goals (SDGs) into daily life.

Responsibilities as Lead Programmer & Game Designer
  • Designed the game's system backbone structure.
  • Optimized audio files that lagged the game.
  • Implemented and voiced the tutorial scene to guide new players.
  • Created simple texture skins to enrich player experience.
  • Designed gameplay that allowed engaging experience for players.
  • We were asked to participate in CVR2016 to showcase our game at a booth.

Tower of Saviors

A game inspired by 2015 VRGJ Hackathon hosted by Archiact Interactive. In a two man team, we created a futuristic time traveling first person tower defense game. In the not so distant future, we discovered a way to time travel. However due to the state of future, many proposed to change the past to change the future, they were known as the “Shifters”. Those who opposed that notion were called the “Senturies”. To prevent the “Shifters” from changing history, the “Senturies” set up bases at historically important places. The “Senturies” send spotters who uses time traveling VR machine to spot enemies who try to invade them. Prevent them from invading your base and taking over the generator. That is your job.
